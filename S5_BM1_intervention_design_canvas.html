Policy trap check

Run your intervention through the five traps. Flag any that apply and describe how you will address them.

Trap 01Scaling too fastNot checked

Does your design assume the first move will be replicated at scale before results are confirmed? Is there political pressure to scale immediately?

Trap 02Optimising for visibilityNot checked

Is the structural element of your intervention the most visible part — or is it invisible while a more photogenic surface component gets the attention and resources?

Trap 03Ignoring system delaysNot checked

Have you specified how long this type of structural change takes in this system — and communicated that timeline to stakeholders who control the intervention's continuity?

Trap 04The single-pillar fixNot checked

Does your intervention depend on performance in adjacent pillars that are currently weak? Have you accounted for the ceiling those adjacent weaknesses will impose?

Trap 05No feedback mechanismNot checked

Is there a specific, designed feedback mechanism built into the intervention from the start — not added later as an evaluation?
